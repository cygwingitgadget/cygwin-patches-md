**Thread**: [thread](13-46-15.thread.md#:~:text=2020/10/16/13-46-15)

In autogen.sh, use 'aclocal --system-acdir' rather than 'aclocal --acdir'.

'--acdir' was deprecated in automake 1.11 and removed in automake 1.13.
