**Thread**: [thread](20-50-00-1.thread.md#:~:text=2020/01/16/20-50-00-3)

Up to now, opening a symlink with O_NOFOLLOW fails with ELOOP.
Following Linux, allow this to succeed if O_PATH is also specified.
