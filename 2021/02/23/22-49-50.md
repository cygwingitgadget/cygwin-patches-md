**Thread**: [thread](22-49-49.thread.md#:~:text=2021/02/23/22-49-50)

This is in the spirit of the Linux requirement that file operations
like fchmod(2), fchown(2), and fgetxattr(2) fail with EBADF on files
opened with O_PATH.
