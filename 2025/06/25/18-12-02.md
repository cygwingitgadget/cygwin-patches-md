Hi,

Apologies for this minor mistake in the implementation of final `ldp` instruction [https://cygwin.com/pipermail/cygwin-patches/2025q2/013833.html].
It caused a regression in pthread-related tests on our testing environment.
This patch corrects the register load order and aligns it with the descending stack growth convention in AArch64.
