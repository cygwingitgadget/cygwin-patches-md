**Thread**: [thread](14-46-07.thread.md#:~:text=2025/04/11/14-46-07)

In rare cases, '/bin/kill -f PID' hangs because kill(2) is always tried 
first. With this patch, this could be prevented with '/bin/kill -f -s - 
PID'.
