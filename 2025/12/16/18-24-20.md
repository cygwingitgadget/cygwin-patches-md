OSC Ps ; Pt ST"

"o Some controls (such as OSC) introduce a string mode, which is ended on a ST 
(string terminator)."

https://invisible-island.net/xterm/ctlseqs/ctlseqs.html#h3-C1-lparen-8-Bit-rparen-Control-Characters
