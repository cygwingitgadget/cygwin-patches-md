**Thread**: [thread](12-18-18.thread.md#:~:text=2022/02/03/12-18-18)

- Currently, escape sequence IL/DL (CSI Ps L, CSI Ps M) does not
```
  work correctly at the last (bottom end) line. This patch fixes
  the issue.

```
