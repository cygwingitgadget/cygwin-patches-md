**Thread**: [thread](19-15-00.thread.md#:~:text=2019/04/14/19-16-00-8)

If NtReadFile returns STATUS_PENDING, wait for the read to complete.
This can happen, for instance, in the case of a FIFO opened with
O_RDRW.
