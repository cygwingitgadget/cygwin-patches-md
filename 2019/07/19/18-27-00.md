**Thread**: [thread](18-27-00.thread.md#:~:text=2019/07/19/18-27-00)

The recent port to GCC 8 used the 'nonstring' attribute, which is
unknown to GCC 7.  Define and use an 'ATTRIBUTE_NONSTRING' macro
instead.
