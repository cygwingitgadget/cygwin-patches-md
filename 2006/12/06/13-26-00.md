On Linux:
$ gcc -E - <<\EOF | tail -n 2
> #include<sys/ioctl.h>
> TIOCGWINSZ
> EOF

# 2 "<stdin>" 2
0x5413

On cygwin, prior to this patch:
$ gcc -E - <<\EOF | tail -n 2
> #include<sys/ioctl.h>
> TIOCGWINSZ
> EOF

# 2 "<stdin>" 2
TIOCGWINSZ

Should be safe to apply since neither TIOCGWINSZ nor <sys/ioctl.h> are
specified by POSIX, so we don't have to worry about namespace pollution.
