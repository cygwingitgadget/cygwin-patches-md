The idea here is to make debugging using a coredump work as usual on a unix,
e.g.:

```
$ ulimit -c unlimited

$ ./segv-program
*** starting 'dumper "C:/cygwin64/work/segv-program.exe" 3712' for pid 546, tid 10640
```


$ gdb segv-program.exe segv-program.exe.core
[...]
